Numbering is sequential across all plans/phases — append, never renumber.

projects (multiple parallel lines of inquiry, each in its own
`insights/<theme>/` subfolder), keep the column and link relative
paths from `INDEX.md` (`[Title](spatial-equilibrium/01_slug.md)`).
